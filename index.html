theStudio

Welcome to the studio. You don't need any brushes. Just click some buttons. Feel free to move the painting around on the wall. You are the artist.

The three elements of the painting are the solid color background, a middleground composed of Hans Hoffman-esque rectangular “figures”, and a foreground of the lines and gestures in the painting.

If you aren't feeling creative, the program will make a canvas for you and paint some nice modern lines and shapes. Just click “I don't like decisions”. It's okay, I don't either. Art is hard. Otherwise, click “Rectangles!” a few times, and maybe add some lines. It's up to you.

Values must be greater than 50 and less than 900
